OBSERVING

Are you looking for Mass Defense support? If you’d like to request Legal Observers® (LOs) for an action or learn about other forms of social movement mass defense support, scroll down to reach out to your nearest NLG chapter! If you cannot get in contact or have any questions, you can email massdef@nlg.org to reach our Director of Mass Defense. However, each individual chapter dispatches their own LOs.

REQUEST LEGAL OBSERVERS

NLG Rochster provides legal observers upon request for activists. Email us at Rochester@NLG.org to request legal observers.

MASS DEFENSE PROGRAM

The National Lawyers Guild Mass Defense Program is a network of activists, community members, organizers, legal workers, law students, and lawyers providing legal support for protests and movements taking an abolitionist, anti-racist, anti-capitalist, and anti-imperialist approach to human rights work.
