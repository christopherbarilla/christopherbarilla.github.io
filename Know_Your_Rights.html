KNOW YOUR

RIGHTS

NLG Rochester supporters activists with issues related to our legal system. One important component of that support is informing people of their rights as it relates to law enforcement, employers, and the court system. NLG regularly published Know Your Rights guides. NLG Rochester has also hosted Know Your Rights trainings both publicly and privately by request. If you would like to request a Know Your Rights Training for an upcoming protest, union campaign, or any action that would benefit from a legal perspective you can contact us and request a training.

Know Your Rights Training

We provide Know Your Rights trainings for groups and organizations tailored to specific actions. It is important to know your rights while planning an action and we can help with that. You can email us or call us to request a training.
