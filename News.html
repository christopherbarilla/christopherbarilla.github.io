We at NLG believe people have the right to publicly voice thier political beliefs and protest. The state at times in fringes on these rights and that is where we step in. Legal Observers keep an eye on police and law enforcement officials at public protests at the request of protest organizers. Legal observers wear green NLG hats to clearly identify themselves as legal observers and watch the cops. The observers are their to witness and document actions by law enforcement.
